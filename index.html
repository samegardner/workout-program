Nutrition + Supplements

What to eat, what to buy
❯

Daily Targets

  • Calories: 2,200-2,400/day (slight deficit for recomp)
  • Protein: 140-150g/day (non-negotiable)
  • Carbs: 200-250g/day (fuel for lifting + running)
  • Fat: 55-70g/day

Buy These

  • Whey protein — 1 scoop post-workout. ON Gold Standard or Dymatize ISO100.
  • Creatine monohydrate — 5g/day, every day. ~$15 for 2 months. Plain unflavored.

Nice to Have

  • Pre-workout or caffeine pills — 200-300mg, 30 min before. Or just coffee.
  • Vitamin D3 — 2000-5000 IU/day.

Progression + Recovery

How to get stronger every week
❯

Ramp-Up (Weeks 1-4)

  • Weeks 1-2: ~60-65% effort. Nail the form.
  • Week 3: ~80% effort. Find your working weights.
  • Week 4+: Full working weights. Double progression begins.

Muscle memory is real. Expect rapid gains for 4-8 weeks.

Double Progression

Compounds (bench, squat, rows, OHP):

  • Start at a weight you can do for sets of 6
  • Add reps each session until you hit sets of 8
  • Add 5 lbs (upper) or 10 lbs (lower), reset to 6
  • Stalled 2+ sessions? Drop 10%, rebuild

Accessories: Same idea, smallest increment at the top of the rep range.

4-Week Targets

  • Bench: +15-20 lbs
  • Squat: +20-30 lbs
  • Rows: +15-20 lbs
  • OHP: +10-15 lbs

Recovery

  • Saturday + Sunday: Full rest.
  • Sleep: 7-9 hours. Non-negotiable.
  • Beat up mid-week? Cut the 5K or drop 1-2 sets.

Warmup (Every Session, 5 min)

Arm circles (15 each way) → Band pull-aparts (15) → Hip circles (10 each) → Bodyweight squats (10) → 1 light set of first exercise at 50%
